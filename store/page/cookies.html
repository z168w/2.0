Cookies

This website uses cookies. A cookie is a small text file that can be stored on your computer's hard drive if you agree. It may be stored and updated at various times as you access the website. Each time you visit the website, the server may deliver certain customised information to your computer based on the data stored in your cookie. Cookies do not identify people, but rather they are defined themselves by a combination of a computer, a user account, and a browser.

First Party Cookies

We never use cookies to retrieve information from your computer which was not originally contained in the cookie.
